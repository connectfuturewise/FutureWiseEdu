PROGRAMMES

Data Analytics

Our Data Analysis program equips students with the skills to turn raw numbers into powerful insights. Master data visualization, statistical analysis, and business intelligence tools like Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to analyze, visualize, and present data like a pro—skills in high demand across every industry today.

Product Designing & Prototyping

Equips students to turn ideas into functional, user-centric solutions. Master 3D modeling, CAD design, and prototyping using Fusion 360, SolidWorks, and Adobe XD to design, test, and refine products—skills highly sought after in tech, manufacturing, and innovation-driven industries today.

IoT & Electronics

Equips students to build smart, connected devices from concept to prototype. Master microcontrollers, sensors, and IoT platforms like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and ESP32 to design, program, and deploy intelligent systems—preparing you to innovate in automation, smart homes, wearable tech, and next-generation electronics.

Programming & Software Development

Gain the skills to transform ideas into functional, efficient software solutions. Learn Python, Java, and C++, along with frameworks and tools to build, test, and deploy applications—preparing you to innovate in software engineering, app development, and cutting-edge tech industries.
